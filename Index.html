Basotho Foundation

A Basotho foundation is not a singular entity, but rather refers to various charitable and non-governmental organizations (NGOs) in Lesotho that aim to empower Basotho people, particularly youth and vulnerable communities, through education, skills development, and economic empowerment initiatives.They were all gathered by Moshoeshoe from various culters to make one nation.
